Exercise 4

In this exercise, we will respond to element events and remove a shopping checklist item from the list of items on the page. We will use "shoplist.html" as a starting point and proceed with the following steps:

  1. When the checkbox is clicked, update the complete state of the checklist item.
  2. When the delete button is clicked, remove the item from the checklist and update the page.
  3. What improvements would you suggest?
    • Initial focus
    • Reset entry form
    • Check for duplicate items
